Welcome to Shri.K. J. Polytechnic College

The Shri.K. J. Polytechnic College is situated in Bharuch City of Gujarat State.
It is 6 KM from nearby the railway station and 2 KM from the nearby bus station. So it is an ideal choice for students from all over INDIA, especially from Gujarat.
It is one of the topmost colleges in Gujarat for pursuing Diploma courses. It provides a very pleasant atmosphere to students.
